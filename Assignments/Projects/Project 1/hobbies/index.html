Webcomics

One of the things I love to do most is read webcomics. There are so many worlds that just come alive with words and still images. With webcomics, you get a much more vivid picture of what the author had in mind, yet you also still get to imagine what happens between panels, what the characters sounds like, and a variety of other things. It truly offers a unique experience that I haven't yet found anywhere else.
